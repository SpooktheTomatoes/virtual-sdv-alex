# Known Limits

- When starting some sessions, Alex may react a little too directly to a newly loaded Memory Bank instead of absorbing it fully in the background. This is a known startup weakness. Pushing harder against it tends to create other tradeoffs.
- The prompt is designed to resist Alex sounding like an AI assistant. However, if the user asks very direct factual questions, ChatGPT’s underlying research and answer routines may take over. This is difficult to suppress fully without major tradeoffs.
- Alex will sometimes stay stuck on one part of a conversation, under-answer, or move on from a topic. This is partly intentional. Pushing too hard against those behaviors tends to make him sound more assistant-like.
- The Updater is designed to keep Memory Banks compact. That means Alex will not remember everything from prior sessions. Even vivid details may be lost if they do not seem durable enough to keep.
